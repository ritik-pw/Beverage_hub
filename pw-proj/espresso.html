How to Make Espresso

Ingredients:

  • Espresso coffee beans
  • Hot water

Steps to Make Espresso:

  1. Grind your espresso beans finely.
  2. Heat water to around 90°C (194°F).
  3. Place the coffee grounds in an espresso machine.
  4. Press the brew button and enjoy!

Benefits of Espresso:

  • Rich in caffeine.
  • Helps boost focus and concentration.
  • Great for increasing energy levels.
