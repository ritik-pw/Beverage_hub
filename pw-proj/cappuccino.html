How to Make a Cappuccino

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Steamed milk
  • Milk foam
  • Cocoa powder (optional)

Steps:

  1. Brew a shot of espresso.
  2. Steam milk to create a thick foam.
  3. Pour equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.
  4. Sprinkle cocoa powder on top.

Benefits:

  • Good source of protein and calcium.
  • Enhances alertness and focus.
  • Provides a creamy, rich flavor.
