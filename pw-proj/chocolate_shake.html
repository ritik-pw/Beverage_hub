How to Make Chocolate Shake

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar

Steps to Make Chocolate Shake:

  1. Blend milk and cocoa powder.
  2. Add sugar to taste.
  3. Blend again until smooth and creamy.
  4. Serve chilled with ice cubes and enjoy!

Benefits of Chocolate Shake:

  • Rich in protein and calcium.
  • Boosts energy levels.
  • Delicious and satisfying treat.
