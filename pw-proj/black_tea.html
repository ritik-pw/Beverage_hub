How to Make Black Tea

Ingredients:

  • Black tea leaves
  • Hot water

Steps to Make Black Tea:

  1. Boil water and add black tea leaves.
  2. Let it steep for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Strain and serve. Enjoy your cup of Black Tea!

Benefits of Black Tea:

  • High in caffeine.
  • Improves focus and alertness.
  • Helps in reducing the risk of stroke.
