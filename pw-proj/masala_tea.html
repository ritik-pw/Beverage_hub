How to Make Masala Tea

Ingredients:

  • Black tea leaves
  • Milk
  • Spices (cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, cloves)

Steps to Make Masala Tea:

  1. Boil water with spices for 5 minutes.
  2. Add tea leaves and let it simmer for a couple of minutes.
  3. Pour in milk and boil for another 2 minutes.
  4. Strain, and enjoy your flavorful Masala Tea!

Benefits of Masala Tea:

  • Boosts immunity.
  • Helps with digestion.
  • Contains anti-inflammatory properties.
