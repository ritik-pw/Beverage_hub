How to Make a Mango Shake

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar or honey
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps:

  1. Peel and chop the mango.
  2. Add mango, milk, sugar, and ice cubes to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.

Benefits:

  • Rich in vitamins A and C.
  • Boosts immunity and digestion.
  • A sweet and refreshing summer drink.
