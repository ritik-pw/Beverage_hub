How to Make a Strawberry Shake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 2 scoops vanilla or strawberry ice cream
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop fresh strawberries.
  2. Add strawberries, milk, ice cream, and honey to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and top with whipped cream.

Benefits:

  • Rich in vitamins and antioxidants.
  • Boosts immunity and skin health.
  • Delicious and naturally sweet.
