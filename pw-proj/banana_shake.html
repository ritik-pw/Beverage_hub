How to Make a Banana Shake

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Peel and chop the banana.
  2. Add banana, milk, honey, and ice cream to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.

Benefits:

  • Rich in potassium and fiber.
  • Boosts energy and digestion.
  • A naturally sweet and nutritious drink.
