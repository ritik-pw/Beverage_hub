How to Make a Mocha

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Steamed milk
  • Chocolate syrup or cocoa powder
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Brew a shot of espresso.
  2. Mix espresso with chocolate syrup or cocoa powder.
  3. Steam the milk and pour it into the espresso mixture.
  4. Top with whipped cream if desired.

Benefits:

  • Delicious combination of coffee and chocolate.
  • Provides an energy boost.
  • Rich in antioxidants.
