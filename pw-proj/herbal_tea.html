How to Make Herbal Tea

Ingredients:

  • Herbs (e.g., chamomile, mint)
  • Hot water

Steps to Make Herbal Tea:

  1. Boil water and pour it over the herbs.
  2. Let it steep for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Strain and serve hot or cold.

Benefits of Herbal Tea:

  • Caffeine-free.
  • Good for digestion.
  • Helps with relaxation and stress relief.
