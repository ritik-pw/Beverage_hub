How to Make an Americano

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Hot water

Steps:

  1. Brew a shot of espresso.
  2. Boil water separately.
  3. Pour hot water over the espresso in a 1:2 or 1:3 ratio.
  4. Stir and enjoy.

Benefits:

  • Lower caffeine concentration than espresso.
  • Rich and smooth taste.
  • Hydrating and refreshing.
